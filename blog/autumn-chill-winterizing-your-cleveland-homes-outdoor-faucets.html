Autumn Chill: Winterizing Your Cleveland Home's Outdoor Faucets

With Cleveland's autumn chill arriving, proactively winterizing your outdoor faucets is essential to protect your home from costly pipe damage. Ensure you disconnect all hoses, then locate and close the interior shut-off valve for each outdoor spigot before opening the exterior faucet to drain any residual water.
