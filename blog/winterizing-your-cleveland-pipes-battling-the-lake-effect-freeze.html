Winterizing Your Cleveland Pipes: Battling the Lake Effect Freeze

Cleveland's brutal Lake Effect winters demand proactive pipe protection to avoid costly bursts and water damage. Ensure exposed pipes in unheated areas are well-insulated and consider disconnecting outdoor hoses and shutting off their interior valves before the deep freeze arrives.
