Winter's Wrath: Thawing Cleveland's Frozen Pipes

When Cleveland's winter wrath strikes and you suspect a frozen pipe, never use an open flame to thaw it, as this poses a severe fire risk and can damage your plumbing. Instead, safely apply gentle heat with a hairdryer or heating pad, and if you're unsure, always contact a local plumbing professional for assistance.
