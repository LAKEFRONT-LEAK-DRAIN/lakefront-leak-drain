Protect Your Plumbing From Lake Erie's Chill

As Cleveland embraces Lake Erie's crisp chill, it's vital to safeguard your plumbing from freezing temperatures. Insulate exposed pipes in unheated areas like basements and garages, and consider letting a small drip run from cold water faucets during extreme freezes to prevent costly bursts.
