Lake Effect Pipe Protection: Cleveland's Guide to Preventing Winter Bursts

Cleveland's lake effect winters can significantly increase the risk of pipes freezing and bursting, so proactive protection is key. To safeguard your home, ensure all exterior hose bibs are fully drained and insulated, and consider adding extra insulation to exposed pipes in unheated areas like basements and crawl spaces.
