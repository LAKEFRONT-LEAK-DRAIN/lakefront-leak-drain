How to Spot a Hidden Leak

If this issue is urgent (active leak, flooding, no hot water), call/text 216-505-7765 for the fastest help.

What homeowners usually notice

  • Changes in water pressure, odors, or slow drains
  • Moisture, staining, or unexplained water
  • Noise from pipes, sump pumps, or water heaters

What you can do right now

  • Know where your main shutoff is and how to use it
  • Protect floors with towels/buckets and reduce water use
  • For winter issues, keep cabinets open and run a small trickle

When to call a pro

If the problem is recurring, causing damage, or you’re unsure what’s happening, it’s time to get professional help.
