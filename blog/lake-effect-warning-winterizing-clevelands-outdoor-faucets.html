Lake Effect Warning: Winterizing Cleveland's Outdoor Faucets

Cleveland's lake effect winters necessitate proper winterization of outdoor faucets to prevent burst pipes and costly damage. Be sure to disconnect all hoses, drain your exterior lines, and apply insulated covers for thorough protection against freezing temperatures.
