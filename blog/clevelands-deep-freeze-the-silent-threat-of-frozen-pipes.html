Cleveland's Deep Freeze: The Silent Threat of Frozen Pipes

Cleveland's deep freezes silently threaten your pipes, so ensure exposed plumbing in unheated areas like basements and garages is well-insulated before temperatures plummet. Additionally, letting a small trickle of water run from faucets farthest from your main water supply can help prevent freezing by maintaining water movement through your system.
