Cleveland Winter Watch: Protecting Pipes from the Freeze

To prepare for Cleveland's freezing temperatures, properly insulate exposed pipes, especially those in unheated areas like basements and garages. During extreme cold, consider opening cabinet doors under sinks and letting a trickle of water run from a faucet to help prevent pipes from freezing and bursting.
