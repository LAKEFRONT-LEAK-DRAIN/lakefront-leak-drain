Cleveland's Winter Chill: Prevent Frozen Pipes

To combat Cleveland's harsh winter chill and prevent costly frozen pipes, take proactive steps before temperatures plummet. Ensure outdoor spigots are drained and disconnected, and insulate any exposed pipes in unheated areas like basements or crawl spaces.
