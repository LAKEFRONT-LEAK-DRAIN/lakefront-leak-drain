Cleveland's Deep Freeze: Don't Let Lake Effect Snow Burst Your Pipes

Cleveland's deep freeze and lake effect snow can put your pipes at serious risk for bursting, so taking proactive steps is crucial to protect your home. Ensure your outdoor spigots are fully drained and insulated, and consider letting indoor faucets drip a tiny stream of cold water during extreme cold snaps to maintain water flow and prevent freezing.
