Cleveland Winter Prep: Protecting Outdoor Faucets from the Big Chill

Before Cleveland's winter truly sets in, ensure you disconnect all hoses from outdoor spigots and locate the interior shut-off valve for each. Turn off the water supply to these exterior lines, then open the outdoor faucets to drain them completely, preventing burst pipes from freezing temperatures.
