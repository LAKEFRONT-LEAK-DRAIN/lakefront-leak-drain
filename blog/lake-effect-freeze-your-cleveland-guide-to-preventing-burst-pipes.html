Lake Effect Freeze: Your Cleveland Guide to Preventing Burst Pipes

As Cleveland experiences lake effect freezes, ensure all exposed pipes in unheated areas like basements and crawl spaces are properly insulated to guard against the extreme cold. Additionally, during severe temperature drops, consider letting a faucet farthest from your main water shut-off drip slowly to maintain water flow and relieve pressure, significantly reducing your risk of burst pipes.
