Cleveland's Winter Plumbing: Don't Get Frozen Out!

As Cleveland winters approach, protect your home by insulating exposed pipes in unheated areas like basements and crawl spaces. Additionally, remember to disconnect and drain all outdoor hoses to prevent water from freezing and causing costly pipe bursts.
