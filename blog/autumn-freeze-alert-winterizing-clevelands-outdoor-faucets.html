Autumn Freeze Alert: Winterizing Cleveland's Outdoor Faucets

Cleveland, with autumn upon us, it's crucial to winterize your outdoor faucets now to prevent burst pipes when the deep freeze hits. Be sure to disconnect all hoses, drain the remaining water, and turn off the interior shut-off valve for each outdoor spigot.
