Summer Sludge: Preventing AC Drain Clogs in Humid Cleveland

Cleveland's humid summers can cause "summer sludge" to build up in your AC's condensate drain line, leading to inconvenient and potentially damaging clogs. To prevent this, regularly pour a cup of distilled white vinegar down the condensate line, typically found near your outdoor unit or in your attic, to clear out algae and debris.
