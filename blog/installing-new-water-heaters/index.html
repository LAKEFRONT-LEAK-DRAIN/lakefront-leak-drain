Installing New Water Heaters

If this issue is urgent (active leak, flooding, no hot water), call/text 216-505-7765 for the fastest help.

What homeowners usually notice

  • Inconsistent or no hot water coming from faucets and showers
  • Rust-colored water or a metallic taste indicating tank corrosion
  • Rumbling or popping noises from an aging water heater

What you can do right now

  • Determine the age of your water heater — most last 8–12 years
  • Choose between tank and tankless models based on your household's hot water needs
  • Shut off the water supply and power/gas to the old unit before any work begins

When to call a pro

Water heater installation involves gas or electrical connections, pressure relief valves, and proper venting — always hire a licensed plumber to ensure safety and code compliance.
