Autumn Freeze Alert: Winterizing Your Cleveland Outdoor Plumbing

As autumn progresses and Cleveland faces colder temperatures, proactively winterizing your outdoor plumbing is essential to prevent costly freeze damage. Be sure to disconnect all garden hoses and drain water from outdoor spigots, considering turning off the water supply to exterior lines if you have a dedicated shut-off valve.
