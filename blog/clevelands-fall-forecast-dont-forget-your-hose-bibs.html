Cleveland's Fall Forecast: Don't Forget Your Hose Bibs!

As Cleveland's fall forecast brings cooler temperatures, remember to prepare your outdoor hose bibs. Disconnect all hoses and, if equipped, turn off their interior shut-off valves to prevent freezing and potential burst pipes.
