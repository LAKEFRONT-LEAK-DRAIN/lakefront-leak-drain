Autumn Leaves & Clogged Drains: A Cleveland Pre-Winter Checklist

As autumn leaves blanket Cleveland, they can easily wash into outdoor drains and storm sewers, creating significant clogs before winter sets in. Before the first freeze, be sure to clear all leaves and debris from your gutters, downspouts, and outdoor drains to prevent ice dams and potential backups.
