Cleveland's Fall Foliage: Protecting Your Drains from Leafy Blockages

As Cleveland's vibrant fall foliage arrives, be proactive in protecting your home by regularly clearing leaves from gutters, downspouts, and storm drains to prevent costly blockages. Consider installing leaf guards on your gutters and drain covers to effectively keep debris out of your plumbing system and avoid potential water damage.
