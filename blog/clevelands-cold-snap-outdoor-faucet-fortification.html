Cleveland's Cold Snap: Outdoor Faucet Fortification

As Cleveland braces for its cold snap, ensuring your outdoor faucets are properly fortified is key to preventing costly winter damage. Disconnect all hoses, drain residual water from the spigot, and if applicable, use an indoor shut-off valve to completely cut off water supply to the exterior line.
