Brrr! Cleveland Winterizing Your Pipes Before the Lake Effect Freeze

Before the Lake Effect Freeze truly sets in, Cleveland residents should prioritize protecting their outdoor plumbing to prevent costly damage. Disconnect and drain all garden hoses, then consider insulating exterior faucets and any exposed pipes with foam covers to brave the winter chill.
