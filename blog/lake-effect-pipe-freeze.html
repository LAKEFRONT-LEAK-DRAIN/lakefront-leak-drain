Lake Effect Pipe Freeze

Cleveland's lake effect amplifies the risk of pipe freezes during extreme cold snaps. To protect your plumbing, keep cabinet doors open under sinks and consider a slow drip from faucets when temperatures plummet below freezing for extended periods.
